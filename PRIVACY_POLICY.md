## Information we collect

The App itself does **not** ask you for personal information and has no login.

We use **Google AdMob** to show advertisements. To serve and measure ads, AdMob
and its partners may collect and process device and usage data, which can
include:

- Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA)
- Device information (model, OS version, language, coarse region)
- App interaction and ad-interaction events
- IP address (used for approximate location and fraud prevention)

We also store your **high score and settings locally on your device**. This data
stays on the device and is not transmitted to us.

## How the information is used

- To display advertising (including, where permitted, personalized ads)
- To measure ad performance and prevent fraud/invalid traffic
- To keep the App working and improve it

We do not sell your personal information.

## Advertising and your choices

Ads are provided by Google AdMob. You can learn how Google uses data, and manage
ad personalization, here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads lets you reset or opt out of your
  advertising ID.
- **iOS:** the App asks for tracking permission (App Tracking Transparency); you
  can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. You may
  still see non-personalized ads.

## Children's privacy

The App is intended for a **general audience** and is not directed to children
under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you
believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will address it.

## Data retention

We do not maintain our own servers of your personal data. Ad-related data is
handled by Google per its retention policies. Local game data is removed when you
uninstall the App or clear its storage.
